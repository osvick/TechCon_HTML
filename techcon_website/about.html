Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of passionate technologists in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference attended by thousands of innovators, developers, and industry leaders. Each year, the conference brings cutting-edge tech discussions and showcases the evolution of digital transformation.
